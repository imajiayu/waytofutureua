Way to Future Ukraine Project Update

Dnipro, Ukraine

When the Power Goes Out, a Hot Meal Means Everything

Since late 2025, sustained Russian strikes on Ukraine's energy infrastructure have made daily power outages the norm. For elderly residents in Dnipro's high-rises — elevators dead, stoves useless, heating gone — a single hot meal can be the difference between getting through the day and not.

Why This Matters

Many elderly residents live alone on modest pensions in high-rise apartments. Without electricity, they cannot cook, heat water, or use elevators. A single trip down the stairs each day pushes them to their physical limits. They have no way to afford eating out and limited access to information about available help.
